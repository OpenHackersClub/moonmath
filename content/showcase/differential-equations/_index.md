Differential equations model change: the Picard–Lindelöf theorem guarantees solutions exist under Lipschitz regularity, classical methods (integrating factors, variation of parameters) produce them explicitly, and fundamental PDEs — heat, wave, Laplace — encode diffusion, vibration, and equilibrium.
