Combinatorics is the art of counting structured collections: how many ways can objects be arranged, selected, or partitioned? These pages develop the foundational principles — from the Pigeonhole Principle to Bell numbers — each illustrated with concrete visualizations and verified Lean4 proofs against Mathlib v4.28.0.
