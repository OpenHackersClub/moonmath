Optimization mathematics studies how to find the best element from a set of candidates under given constraints. These notes cover the foundational theorems — convexity criteria, Lagrange multipliers, KKT conditions, LP duality, the minimax theorem, Nash equilibria, and gradient descent convergence — each with a concrete numerical example and a Lean4 proof.
